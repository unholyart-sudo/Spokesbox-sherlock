04 Logo Usage Rules

✅ Do

  • Use the logo on approved backgrounds (#1a2744 navy, white, or #1a1a2e charcoal)
  • Maintain clear space on all sides equal to the height of the "S" in SPOKESBOX
  • Use the provided logo files — do not recreate from memory
  • Scale the logo proportionally to preserve aspect ratio
  • Use the correct color variation for each context (primary, dark, reversed)

❌ Don't

  • Stretch, squish, or otherwise distort the logo in any direction
  • Change the logo colors outside approved brand palette
  • Add drop shadows, glows, gradients, or decorative effects
  • Place the logo on busy photographic or patterned backgrounds
  • Recreate or redraw the logo — always use official source files
  • Rotate or flip the logo at any angle

Minimum Clear Space

Maintain a clear zone equal to the cap-height of the letter "S" in SPOKESBOX on all four sides of the logo. No other elements may intrude into this zone.

Context Minimum Size
Digital / Screen 120px wide
Print / Offset 1 inch wide
Favicon / App Icon Use Icon Mark only
Large Format / OOH No maximum — use vector

Why This Matters

Below minimum size, the icon's ray detail and tagline become illegible. At very small sizes, use the Icon Mark alone rather than the full lockup.
