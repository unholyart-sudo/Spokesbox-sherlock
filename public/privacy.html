Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Spokesbox ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — required to deliver your newsletter
  • Location (city/region) — used for local weather and news
  • Topic preferences — collected via onboarding wizard and reply updates
  • Reply content — when you reply to emails to update preferences
  • Usage data — email open/click rates via SendGrid (aggregate only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and send your personalized daily newsletter
  • To process preference updates when you reply to emails
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, confirmations)
  • To improve the service through aggregate analytics
  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never use your data for advertising targeting

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and apply access controls to limit who can access subscriber data.

4. Reply Content & AI Processing

When you reply to a Spokesbox email with a preference update (e.g., "Add more tech news"), that reply is processed by an AI language model to extract your instruction. Reply content is:

  • Screened automatically for inappropriate content before processing
  • Used only to update your personal preferences
  • Not shared with other subscribers or third parties
  • Retained for up to 90 days for service improvement, then deleted

5. Content Moderation

All user-submitted content (including reply instructions) is automatically screened. Requests involving adult content, illegal activities, harassment, or other prohibited content are rejected and not processed. Repeated violations may result in account suspension.

6. Third-Party Services

  • SendGrid — email delivery
  • OpenAI — AI content generation and moderation
  • Open-Meteo / wttr.in — weather data

Each third party has their own privacy policy governing their data handling.

7. Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe — click the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Data deletion — email us and we will delete your account within 30 days
  • Data access — request a copy of your stored data at any time
  • Correction — update your preferences by replying to any newsletter

EU/EEA residents have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management on the web interface only. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

9. Children's Privacy

Spokesbox is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated by email. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance.
