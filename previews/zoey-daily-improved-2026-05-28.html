🎨 Art Technique of the Day

Salt Painting Magic! ✨

You'll need: white glue, table salt, watercolor paints, card stock or thick paper.

  1. Draw a shape with glue — a star, heart, or rainbow works great!
  2. While the glue is still wet, sprinkle salt all over it. Shake off the extra.
  3. Wait 2 minutes. Then dip a wet paintbrush in watercolor and GENTLY touch the salt — watch the color spread by magic! 🌈
  4. Try different colors in different spots. The salt does the blending for you!
