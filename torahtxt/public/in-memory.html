In Loving Memory

Michael Green

of Blessed Memory
זכרונו לברכה
Michael Green surrounded by the family he loved

Michael believed that no one should ever be left out — whether at a holiday table or in the community. Like the patriarchs, he ran toward acts of kindness, serving his neighbors in Fayetteville and beyond with a joyful heart. We honor his lifelong dedication to philanthropy and his unwavering love for his family and the Jewish people.
