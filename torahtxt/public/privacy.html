Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

What We Collect

When you sign up for TorahTxt, we collect your name and phone number. We use this information solely to send you daily Torah wisdom text messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used exclusively to deliver TorahTxt daily messages. We do not sell, rent, share, or use your information for marketing purposes or share it with any third parties.

Message Frequency

You will receive one (1) SMS message per day, delivered at approximately 6:30am ET.

How to Opt Out

You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP to any TorahTxt message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Data Storage

Your name and phone number are stored securely on our servers. We retain this data only as long as you are an active subscriber. Upon unsubscribing, your data is deactivated.

SMS Carrier Fees

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details.

Contact

Questions? Email us at info@torahtxt.com
