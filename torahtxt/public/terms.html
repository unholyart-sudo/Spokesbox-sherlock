Program Description

TorahTxt is a free daily SMS subscription service delivering Torah wisdom, D'var Torah, and Jewish learning directly to your phone. Messages are sent once per day at approximately 6:30am ET.

Opt-In

By submitting your name and phone number at torahtxt.com, you agree to receive one (1) SMS message per day from TorahTxt. You confirm you are the owner or authorized user of the phone number provided.

Opt-Out / STOP

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. You may also reply HELP for support information. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message.

Message and data rates may apply. TorahTxt is free to join but your wireless carrier may charge for SMS/MMS messages received.
